Грамматика Словарь Тест

Времена Английского

PRESENT (Настоящее) ▼

Simple

Факт. V / V-s
I study every day.

Continuous

Сейчас. am/is/are + V-ing
I am studying now.

Perfect

Результат. have/has + V3
I have studied.

Perfect Continuous

Длительность. have been + V-ing
I have been studying.

PAST (Прошлое) ▼

Simple

В прошлом. V2 / -ed
I studied.

Continuous

Процесс. was/were + V-ing
I was studying.

Perfect

До момента. had + V3
I had studied.

Perfect Continuous

Долго до момента. had been + V-ing
I had been studying.

FUTURE (Будущее) ▼

Simple

Будет. will + V
I will study.

Continuous

Будет длиться. will be + V-ing
I will be studying.

Perfect

Сделаю к... will have + V3
I will have studied.

Perfect Continuous

Буду учить уже... will have been + V-ing
I will have been studying.

FUTURE IN THE PAST ▼

Simple

would + V

Continuous

would be + V-ing

Perfect

would have + V3

Perfect Continuous

would have been + V-ing
