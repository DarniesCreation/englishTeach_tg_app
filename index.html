Грамматика (16)

PRESENT (Настоящее) ▼

Simple

Факт или привычка.

I/You/We/They + V || He/She/It + V-s I study every day || He studies every day

Continuous

Сейчас происходит.

I + am || He/She/It + is || You/We/They + are + V-ing I am studying now.

Perfect

Результат к настоящему моменту.

I/You/We/They + have || He/She/It + has + V3 I have studied already.

Perfect Continuous

Началось тогда и всё еще идет.

I/You/We/They + have || He/She/It + has + been + V-ing I have been studying for 2 hours.
PAST (Прошлое) ▼

Simple

Завершено в прошлом.

I/He/She/It/You/We/They + V2 (-ed) I studied yesterday.

Continuous

Процесс в конкретный момент прошлого.

I/He/She/It + was || You/We/They + were + V-ing I was studying at 5 PM.

Perfect

Случилось до другого события в прошлом.

All subjects + had + V3 I had studied before he arrived.

Perfect Continuous

Длилось до определенного момента в прошлом.

All subjects + had been + V-ing I had been studying for an hour when she called.
FUTURE (Будущее) ▼

Simple

Прогноз или решение.

All subjects + will + V I will study tomorrow.

Continuous

Процесс в будущем.

All subjects + will be + V-ing I will be studying all night.

Perfect

Будет завершено к сроку.

All subjects + will have + V3 I will have studied by 6 PM.

Perfect Continuous

Длительность к моменту в будущем.

All subjects + will have been + V-ing I will have been studying for 5 years in 2026.
FUTURE IN THE PAST ▼

Simple

Будущее из прошлого.

would + VI said I would study.

Continuous

would be + V-ingHe said he would be studying.

Perfect

would have + V3I would have studied.

Perfect Continuous

would have been + V-ingI would have been studying.
